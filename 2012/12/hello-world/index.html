    Hello World!

    We’re alive in the blogverse!

    From now on we’ll be posting here our progress throughout the development of the project. Expect us to start with a few posts to catch up with everything that has been going on with ParticleQuest since the beginning.

    Just to summarize and spoil some of the upcoming posts, at the moment, we, @OmeGak and @L4D15, are participating in the CUSL, an open source contest, with ParticleQuest. This was pretty much the main motivation for us to work on the project, besides for the fun of hacking, but CERN showed interest in it and Mozilla suggested us to participate in the GameOn as well, so this is just the start a long journey!

    One final announce. The DNS are not updated yet but you will be able to access the blog at http://blog.particlequest.com pretty soon.
